Welcome to the Sorting Hat

This is not a test — there are no right or wrong answers. The Hat will ask you 12 questions about how you see the world, what puzzles interest you, and what kind of researcher you want to become. At the end, it will suggest which paradigm track fits you best.

You can take this as many times as you like. Your result may change as you learn more — that's the point.
