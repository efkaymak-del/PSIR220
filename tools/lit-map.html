What is a Visual Literature Map? It's a spatial representation of your sources organized by school of thought. Instead of listing sources, you arrange them to show who agrees, who disagrees, where the debates are, and where your research fits in the gaps. Think of it as a bird's-eye view of the conversation your paper is entering.
